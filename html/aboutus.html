Our Story

Built by builders,
for builders.

We started with a simple idea: PC building shouldn't require a computer science degree. Today, we help millions turn their dream rigs into reality.

Why we exist

In 2019, a group of PC enthusiasts grew tired of spreadsheets, compatibility headaches, and price-hunting across a dozen tabs. We built the tool we wished we had — and never looked back.

Compatibility First

Our engine checks 200+ rules so you don't have to worry about socket mismatches or PSU wattage.

Real Savings

We track prices across 30+ retailers and alert you when components hit their lowest price.

Community Driven

Every build list, benchmark, and review comes from real builders — not sponsored content.

Our journey

From a weekend project to a platform millions trust.

2019
The Spark

Alex builds the first compatibility checker as a side project after helping friends pick parts.

2020
Going Live

BuildBench launches with 5,000 products and basic price tracking. 100K builders join in the first month.

2022
Scaling Up

What we believe

The principles that guide every feature we ship.

Transparency

No hidden affiliate bias. We show you every retailer, every price, and every compatibility warning — good or bad.

Accessibility

PC building should be for everyone. We design for first-timers as carefully as we do for seasoned enthusiasts.

Accuracy

Our compatibility database is updated daily. When specs change, we change with them — immediately.
